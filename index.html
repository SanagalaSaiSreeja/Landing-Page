About Us

Vignan's Nirula Institute of Technology and Science for Women, Guntur was established in the year 2008 under the aegis of Lavu Educational Society and headed by experienced academician and visionary Dr. Lavu Rathaiah. Vignan’s Nirula marks the most sought after choice for girl students aspiring to pursue quality engineering education. Splendid Avenue, imposing buildings, and a hassle free ride close to 5 minutes from Guntur make it one of the most preferred options for the aspirants. The technical campus, with its competent professional team marches a head to develop high quality, professionally groomed engineering man power through the exploration of ideas, their interpretation, application and dissemination of knowledge. Considering the high expectation of the ever challenging industry, Vignan’s Nirula promises to transform the students in to innovative and dynamic professionals.

Campus Life

Experience a vibrant campus life with various clubs, events, and activities.Step onto the sprawling campus of Vignan Nirula Institute of Technology and Science (VNITS), and you're immediately immersed in a dynamic blend of academic rigor, extracurricular excitement, and a vibrant community spirit. At VNITS, we believe in fostering holistic development, ensuring that every student's journey is enriched with memorable experiences beyond the classroom. Typically, campus life at VNITS or any college includes a mix of academic pursuits, extracurricular activities, and social interactions. Here are some aspects you might expect to find at VNITS:

Academics Excellence

Students at VNITS are likely engaged in various academic activities, including attending lectures, participating in laboratory sessions, and working on projects. The campus might have well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, and libraries to support these activities.At the heart of VNITS lies a commitment to academic excellence. With state-of-the-art facilities, renowned faculty members, and innovative teaching methodologies, VNITS provides a nurturing environment where students explore new trends.

Placements

The Placement Cell plays a crucial role in locating job opportunities for Under Graduates and Post Graduates passing out from the college by keeping in touch with reputed firms and industrial establishments. The Placement Cell operates round the year to facilitate contacts between companies and graduates. The number of students placed through the campus interviews is continuously rising. On invitation, many reputed industries visit the institute to conduct interviews.We have been successful in maintaining our high placement statistics.

Sports

Experience the thrill of competitive sports at Vignan Nirula Institute of Technology and Science (VNITS)! Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated coaches provide the perfect platform for students to pursue their athletic passions. From inter-college tournaments to intramural leagues, there's something for everyone to stay active and engaged. Join us and be a part of our vibrant sports community at VNITS!. Our b-teach team won the prizes in district level kho-kho competition. Our NCC team has done numerous amounts of campus across AP.

Beyond academics, VNITS offers a plethora of extracurricular activities designed to ignite passions, hone talents, and foster camaraderie among students. From cultural festivals that celebrate diversity to technical competitions that showcase innovation, there's never a dull moment on campus. Join one of our many student clubs, unleash your creativity in artistic pursuits, or sweat it out on the sports field – the possibilities are endless. we do have various clubs like dance,coding and singing.
